ABOUT

PaletteKnife is an independent UK label presenting limited editions, garments, objects and curated works through a motion-led digital storefront. Each release is produced in considered quantities, balancing visual identity, rarity and material presence. The project values atmosphere, pace and deliberate presentation over fast retail conventions. Products may appear once, return in altered form, or disappear entirely. We aim to create pieces that feel collected rather than simply purchased. Thank you for visiting and supporting independent work built outside template culture.
